Pune

Location: Western Maharashtra
Famous For: Education & student life (Savitribai Phule Pune University, Symbiosis) IT & automobile industries (Infosys, Tata Motors, Cognizant) Pleasant weather year-round Cultural richness (Marathi heritage, forts, festivals)
Vibe: Calm, growing tech hub with a good mix of traditional and modern culture.
Cost of Living: Affordable to moderate Popular Areas: Hinjewadi (IT hub), Kothrud, Viman Nagar, Baner .

Mumbai

Location: Coastal Maharashtra
Famous For: Bollywood and entertainment industry Financial capital of India (NSE, BSE, RBI HQ) Bustling nightlife, street food, sea views
Vibe: Fast-paced, energetic, never sleeps
Cost of Living: High (especially housing)
Popular Areas: Andheri, Bandra, Navi Mumbai, Colaba

Banglore

Location: Karnataka (Southern India)
Famous For: Major IT and startup hub (Infosys, Wipro, Flipkart, Swiggy) Pleasant climate Cosmopolitan culture, cafes, tech meetups Vibe: Chill, tech-forward, progressive
Cost of Living: Moderate to high
Popular Areas: Whitefield, Koramangala, Electronic City, Indiranagar
